Hotel Doris Dey, Benin

Publish date: 2024-07-04
Take a trip and visit the ancient City of Benin, the Edo State Capital and the best welcome is at HOTEL DORIS DEY; an elegant and prestigious hotel in Benin City, the pride of Edo State, the heart beat of Nigeria.
HOTEL ACCOMMODATION: Our accommodation facilities are tastefully and stylishly decorated and furnished with high quality amenities for your comfort and relaxation. All rooms are exquisitely equipped with quality and standard fittings with all our suites having mini bars; the major differences in the rooms are their sizes and level of comfort. Our accommodation facilities are affordable, very comfortable for family on holiday, business, meeting, leisure/tourism, conference and social engagements. The feel of being in your home is what you will get We promise you will not miss the comfort of your home. All rooms and suites features double bed, Telephone, cable TV, Fridge, working desk, standard Wardrobe with long standing wall mirror. Our suites are double rooms with same amenities in each of the rooms while the living room is very spacious with mini bar and high quality furniture with dinning set. These suites are ideal for families on holiday or pleasure seekers. The granite bathroom has state of the art bathtub, shower and water heater as well as other high quality fittings.

Hotel Doris Dey is a budget hotel in Benin, Edo.
