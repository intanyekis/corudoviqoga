How many times have we seen the same boring, clichéd world of superheroes, with an underdog superhero (or heroes) facing a powerful villain intent upon destroying the world? Eventually, as always, the hero defeats the supervillain – always against all odds – and saves the day.

This plot formula has dominated superhero movies for decades.

However, The Boys has been a refreshing change in the genre — introducing superheroes who are more morally gray, and depicting how they can be dangerous for citizens of the world they inhabit.

The Boys shattered records on all fronts, as it was the most-watched series of 2022 on Amazon Prime, streamed for more than 10.2 billion minutes.

Audiences showered love on the "supe series," and critics were decidedly not critical about it — making it one of the highest-rated television series, with an IMDb rating of 8.7 and a Tomatometer rating of 93 percent.

The Boys' cast is no exception; they received praise for their heartfelt efforts, with most of them landing new projects and enjoying advancement in their careers.

The Boys season four has an anticipated release date of June 13th (later this year), and fans are eagerly waiting to discover the evil deeds of superheroes and the little triumphs of humans.

Let’s dive deeper into the details of The Boys' cast (for season four), their roles, and how much wealth they have amassed in their careers so far.
