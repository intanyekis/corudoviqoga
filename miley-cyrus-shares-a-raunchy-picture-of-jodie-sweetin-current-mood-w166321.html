Miley Cyrus Shares a Raunchy Picture of Jodie Sweetin: Current Mood

Publish date: 2024-07-21

Itchin’ for a fight? Miley Cyrus shared a raunchy throwback photo of Fuller House star Jodie Sweetin via Instagram on Friday, March 4 — and the pop star’s fans aren’t too happy about it.

“Current Mood #fullerhouse,” Cyrus, 23, captioned a split-screen photo of the former child star getting up close and personal with an unidentified man that resembles her ex, Cody Herpin. In the risqué photos, Sweetin, 34, smiles suggestively over her shoulder while wearing a low-cut black tank top and bright purple pants.

“She’s moved on, no need to post about this,” one commenter wrote in response to the picture.

“Karma accelerates for those lacking compassion,” another wrote. “You could have built this woman up with kindness instead of your mockery.”

Sweetin previously opened up about her darker years in her tell-all memoir, unSweetined, back in 2009, candidly admitting to years of drug use and drinking.

“I wanted to prove that I could get more trashed than anyone,” she told Us Weekly about her experiences doing ecstasy in high school and cocaine in college. “There were times I did so much coke, I’d be laying there, getting sick and thinking, ‘I’m going to die.’ But I didn’t care.”

The former child star has since cleaned up her act. In January, she got engaged to her boyfriend of two years, Justin Hodak, and showed off her impressive engagement ring on Instagram.

Sweetin has also since reunited with her Full House costars for the Netflix reboot of the popular ’90s sitcom Fuller House, which kicked off in February.

And on Wednesday, March 2, she revealed that she’ll be dancing with Keo Motsepe for her turn on Dancing With the Stars.

“We have just had the first couple of days. It’s so fun!” she said on Good Morning America. “We got through some basic moves of several different styles of dance. We had a lot of fun and he and I get along really well. And he’s really excited that maybe we’ll get the chance to get past maybe week three or four. He’s a phenomenal dancer and just such a doll.”
