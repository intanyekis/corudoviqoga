Lauren Alaina is Engaged! Meet her Fiance Cam Arnold as announced on Grand Ole Opry

Publish date: 2024-08-09

source: People

  • American country singer Lauren Alaina is engaged as she announced it in between her at the Grand Ole Opry performance.
  • The singer first introduced her fiancé Cam Arnold alongside the engagement ring for the first time in November 2022.
  • So who is the fiance of Lauren Alaina, Cameron Arnold?

Dancin’ In The Moonlight singer Lauren Alaina is engaged! The country star spilled the big secret debuting her engagement ring at her performance at the Grand Ole Opry on Saturday night.

How did Lauren Alaina announce her engagement?

The Wildflower singer never fails to surprise her fans. This time she gave a major relationship update amidst her live performance at the Grand Ole Opry. She reported her candid confession to People as,

“It was a night I will never forget. I got to celebrate the best year of my life. I became a member of the Opry family in February, so I thought they should be the first to know I had been asked to be in another family as well.”

Alaina, 28, telling about her big news with fiance Cameron Arnold, 34, continued,

“This is the first time I’m showing Cameron to the world. He has and will continue to be a sacred piece of my life. He keeps me grounded and gives me a piece of a normal everyday life that I didn’t have before him. We have been together for two and a half years, and we are just getting started. I can’t wait to be Mrs. Cameron Scott Arnold. I’m so excited for everyone to know who he and we are. The best is still to come.”

Onstage Saturday evening, Alaina was quick to debut her new ring on her finger with introducing her fiance to the crowd,

“Everybody, this is my fiancé. I told him he had to come out here and we had to get a picture — we’re doing it because this is the circle and I’m going to hold my hand up and do the thing. Make some noise for my future husband everybody!”

Alaina had also uploaded a series of photos on Instagram regarding her big day with a loving caption, paying tribute to her partner. She wrote.

“BRIDE be dang’d, y’all. My best friend, @arnold.cam, asked me to marry him, and I announced it at my favorite place in the world, @opry. I didn’t know happiness and excitement like this existed. I can’t wait to be Mrs. Cameron Scott Arnold. ? #Laurnold”

Who is Cameron Arnold?

Cameron Arnold is the newly engaged fiance of American singer-songwriter Lauren Alaina. The celebrity spouse-to-be Arnold is also a partner in a Nashville-based insurance firm.

Cameron Arnold probably likes to stay out of the media and limelight as his fiancee Alaina revealed while he headed on the stage to kiss his bride-to-be following a big engagement announcement. The Getting Over Him singer added,

“He did not want to do that! He likes to be in the shadows. He was like, ‘You’re not making me come out there’ and I said, ‘You are not not getting a picture in the circle. So — we negotiated and he came out for the picture.”
