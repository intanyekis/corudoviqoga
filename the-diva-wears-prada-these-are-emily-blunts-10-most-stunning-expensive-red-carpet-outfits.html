Emily Blunt has had an exceptional fashion revolution over the years. At the same time, the actress preferred wearing simple sequins and primary colors. Post-2008, A renowned fashion stylist, Jessica Paster, assisted Blunt in transforming her red carpet look by trying on challenging styles and made fearless statements by wearing bold couture outfits. Over the years, the actress has stunned viewers with her modern yet traditional outfits that amazed everyone.

The diva has always been extraordinary on the red carpet, from the crowd's favorite fashion houses like Michael Kors and Alexander McQueen to wearing exclusive brands like Dundas and the Italian label Schiaparelli.

2016 Academy Awards

Embracing unique silhouettes, She walked the red carpet at the 2016 Oscars looking sublime in a pink Prada gown. The blush pink embellished dress is accessorized with a pair of dangling chandelier diamond earrings and a shimmering clutch. The actress had attended the ceremony as a presenter.

2018 Academy Awards

Emily glided into the 2018 Oscars ceremony dressed in all aquamarine and looked ethereal yet edgy. She was dressed in a Schiaparelli Haute Couture look from the same Spring collection. As mentioned by People, the gown was made from aqua silk, polka dot tulle, and lace. Blunt was dressed in matching shoes and Chopard jewelry worth $250,000, including pear-shaped earrings and rings.

2018 Mary Poppins Returns LA Premiere

For the Los Angeles Premiere of Mary Poppins Returns, Emily played the role of a magical nanny as she dressed in an exquisite white gown. Inspired by Russian poetry and the designer's heritage, the Yanina gown featured a plunging V-neckline, voluminous sleeves, and a floor-length sheer tulle length. The outfit was accessorized with White Kites cascade earrings by Stephen Webster.

2020 A Quiet Place Part II LA Premiere

Blunt shed off the tulle and lace to step onto the red carpet in a bold low-cut red leather dress by Alexander McQueen's Resort 2020 collection. The star of the evening, her perfectly sculpted dress was styled with Jimmy Choo shoes and earrings by Jennifer Fisher, as noted by Just Jared.

2015 Met Gala

With the Met Gala theme for 2015, China: Through The Looking Glass, Blunt dazzled in Prada's custom dress and cape. The dark blue dramatic drape complimented the metallic gray color of the dress. Her outfit is styled with a navy blue Prada clutch and a pair of Anna Hu earrings.

2018 Mary Poppins Returns London Premiere

The star stunned everyone when she marched onto the blue carpet at the London Premiere of Mary Poppins Returns at the Royal Albert Hall. According to Vogue, the actress was dressed in a royal blue silk Schiaparelli couture gown with a sweeping train, with an elegant neckline and cuffed sleeves. She paired her outfit with diamond and sapphire earrings to match the aesthetic of the Disney movie.

2019 SAG Awards

Emily Blunt stole the show at the 2019 SAG Awards red carpet with her most eye-catching look. The actress was nominated in two categories and got awarded Best Supporting Actress for A Quiet Place. She was dressed in a custom pink-column dress from the Michael Kors Collection. The unique feature of her dress was the ruffled, structural design that outlined around the arms and neckline and had paired her dress with a white clutch and Sam Edelman Yaro heels in silver.

2018 Governor's Awards

The artist shimmered at the 2018 Governor's Awards dressed in a custom Dundas outfit. It was an embellished gold two-piece with a crop top and a floor-length skirt. Also, it featured lily and panther motifs on the beaded embellishments of the top and skirt. She had matched the look with simple yellow gold earrings and matching rings.

2021 Met Gala

Following the theme of America: A Lexicon in Fashion, she played her inner Hedy Lamarr as the look was inspired by the movie Ziegfeld Girl, as stated by Marie Claire. The Old Hollywood glamor dress was custom-made by Miu Miu and featured a cape attached to a sheer bodice of the glittery dress. The most eye-gazing feature was the sparkling headpiece decorated with a tiara, diamond stars, and oversized pearls.

2019 Met Gala

Inspired by her role as Mary Poppins, the diva went camp dressed in a custom Michael Kors Collection dress. The shimmering gold gown had a deep V-neckline and created a body-hugging silhouette. Along with carrying a matching purse, she also wore a gold floral hat designed by Stephen Jones.

Emily Blunt has continued to glamor the red carpet for many years with her fresh and elegant choices that steal everyone's attention. With every movie premiere and award show, the actress has enhanced her style statement. She was last seen catching everyone's attention during the press tour of her 2021 Disney movie Jungle Cruise.
