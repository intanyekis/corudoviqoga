Maggie Murdaugh, a 22-year-old University of South Carolina student, died mysteriously, and her family has made headlines. Maggie was discovered shot to death at their family hunting camp in South Carolina on June 7, 2022. The case has sparked national interest, and the inquiry is still underway.

Maggie Murdaugh’s parents, Terry and Kennedy Branstetter, have mainly remained out of the spotlight, although her sister, Marian Proctor, recently testified in the case. Her statement has given some light on the timing of events that night and raised suspicions about her Father, Alex Murdaugh’s, participation.

Maggie Murdaugh’s parents, Terry and Kennedy Branstetter, have been in the limelight since the horrific tragedy.Terry is a well-known South Carolina attorney who works for Peters Murdaugh Parker Eltzroth & Detrick (PMPED), a Murdaugh family law practice. Kennedy, on the other hand, is a stay-at-home mom who reared their children and is well-known in the community for her philanthropic activities.

They’ve been married for nearly 30 years and have three children: Maggie, Paul, and Marian. The family was well-known in the neighborhood for their philanthropy and involvement in several humanitarian organizations.

Terry and Kennedy Branstetter have mostly avoided the public glare since the horrific occurrence, preferring to mourn in private. In public speeches, they expressed their gratitude for the immense support they got from the community.

Who Is Maggie Murdaugh’s Sister, Marian Proctor?

Marian Proctor is Maggie Murdaugh’s younger sister and the daughter of Terry and Kennedy Branstetter.Proctor is a recent University of South Carolina graduate seeking a career in marketing.

Marian has remained out of the limelight since the tragedy, choosing to do so. However, she recently testified in the case, putting light on some of the incident’s facts.

Marian’s testimony has created several doubts about the case, notably concerning her Father, Alex Murdaugh’s, participation.Alex was a well-known local attorney who was facing criminal charges at the time of the occurrence.

Details of Marian Proctor’s Testimony Examined

Marian Proctor testified that she was at the family’s hunting lodge on the night of the event.She reported that she heard gunshots and walked outside to check, where she saw the dead of her mother and brother. She was first terrified, fearing the gunman was still on the premises. She immediately concluded, however, that the gunman had fled and called 911.

Marian’s statement shed some light on the sequence of events that night. She added that her father was not present at the lodge at the time of the event and that he had contacted her to ask her to check on her mother and brother. Marian also reported that she had seen her brother, Paul Murdaugh, earlier that day with a pistol, which he claimed he was carrying for protection.
