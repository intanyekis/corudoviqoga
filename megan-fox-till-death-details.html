10 Things Megan Fox Has Shared About Her New Movie, 'Till Death'

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Megan Fox is back in a really big way with the release of her highly anticipated new movie, Till Death. The film sees her taking on a fresh new role that pushes the boundaries of the type of entertainment she normally produces for fans, and her millions of followers are lapping it up.

The return of Megan Fox to the big screen has been a spark to her career, and is being viewed as a revival in many ways. ET Online reports that the content in this particular flick is intense and promises to entertain the masses without a dull moment. Here's what we know about the film so far...

9 The Plot Line

Megan Fox takes fans on a thrilling ride as she takes on the role of Emma, a woman that one day wakes up to find herself handcuffed to her husband's dead body. The couple were supposed to be enjoying a romantic evening at their secluded lake house, but instead, she finds herself trapped with a corpse in the chill of the winter and having to fight off hired killers to stay alive.

8 Megan Warns Fans To Break Up Before You Cheat

Megan Fox says there's a deep, true meaning behind this movie, and that fans should pay close attention. She cautions that fans should always break up with someone before they end up cheating on them. She's quoted as saying; "I don't know if it's very philosophical but a lesson from this movie, break up or get divorced before you cheat." She went on to say; "it doesn't ever work out well for anyone. [Or you] could end up chained to a dead person."

7 This Was A New Type Of Role For Fox

Megan Fox has done a lot of cool things in her life, but this was definitely her first crack at a marriage drama. She expressed that she had never before been part of a movie that involved "grown up" content. She told the press; " I haven't done, like, a grownup movie. I haven't done a traditional thriller or horror movie before. Because Jennifer's Body was not that. It was very much, like, a teen, angsty, dark comedy. And this is not."

6 Machine Gun Kelly Got A Sneak Peek

Till Death was just released to the public, but there was a very special someone that was given exclusive access to a unique sneak peek of the film, and that was Megan Fox's real-life love interest, Machine Gun Kelly.  The musician was given the opportunity to catch a glimpse of the thriller before its wide release and expressed that he enjoyed watching what his girlfriend was up to and watching her talent unfold.

5 Megan Includes Some Advice For Fans

Megan's experience on the set of the movie helped her gain perspective on relationships, and she now has some advice to share with her fans. Her experience in this dark role has her educating fans on how to love one another, saying that she suggests people be "selfless," meaning you have to always prioritize loving the other person where they are, as they are, who they are in this moment. Not try to change people and not project your own insecurities on some other people. She also encouraged fans to become better listeners.

4 There's A Lesson To Be Learned About Toxic Relationships

Till Death may be a fictional thriller, but it also packs a lot of punch by offering some real-life lessons to fans. Megan Fox tells fans that they should rush out of toxic relationships and be "aligned" with themselves when making decisions on whether to stay and make to make a relationship work or to leave it. She tells fans to work on removing their ego from the equation and describes relationships as being "lifelong journeys."

3 Megan's Physical Strength Was Truly Tested

Megan Fox truly tested her physical strength on the set of Till Death, by actually dragging a stuntman's dead weight behind her. He was a heavy gentleman, and she was actually chained to that real person the whole time they were filming. His job was to actually play dead so she was pulling his entire weight around during each scene. She claimed to have pulled him for well over 13 hours at a time, and expressed that in spite of being physically strong, she truly felt the physical challenge this role posed.

2 Megan Thrives In Environment This Movie Provided

Through the exploration of various roles, Megan has revealed that she truly enjoyed the experience that Till Death had to offer. She said she truly thrives in roles that are stunt-heavy and involve a lot of physical stamina. She also revealed to her fans that she doesn't bode well when asked to be in a room, engaging in conversation all day, so she was truly able to shine in the physical challenges and the creative freedom that this role had to offer.

1 Astrology Had Everything To Do With This

The Washington Post reveals that astrology is a huge part of Megan Fox's personal life, and for her, this movie was truly written in the stars. Part of her normal routine is to read tarot cards, and they have long revealed that she would have a "creative renaissance in her 30s." She says this move was "marked in her natal chart," and claims that the timing of this movie was always written in the stars. She revealed that she is; "spiritual to the point where it might annoy certain people,” and this movie, along with her other current roles, were predicted for her future.
