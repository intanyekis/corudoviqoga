Publish date: 2024-06-06

Seeing a little Spanx here and there isn’t totally shocking — and it’s definitely better than getting caught wearing nothing at all! Beth was all smiles as she posed for the cameras, unphased by her flashing moment at the show despite the fact that her pretty powder blue mini rose up when she crossed her legs.

Beth Behrs Brings Prom Date To Tony Awards:

11 years after they hit the senior prom together, Beth and her date, Matt Doyle, looked beyond dapper at the show. Gone was Beth’s bubble gum pink strapless gown — instead, she opted for a baby blue dress that was edgy, sleek and sexy all at the same time. Luckily for the star there was no sign of her Spanx as she hit the red carpet, putting her legs on display thanks to the thigh-grazing hemline of the dress.

Her frock featured a full skirt attached to a form-fitting mini, which fanned out behind the star, keeping the look formal while allowing her to show some skin all at the same time. Silver ankle-strap sandals polished off her look perfectly.

Sure, Spanx are supposed to stay concealed underneath your clothes, but a glimpse here or there isn’t the end of the world. If you’re getting glam in a form-fitting mini, just like Beth, it’s wise to rely on shapewear to smooth you out — and you won’t have to worry about visible panty lines. We recommend paying extra attention to the hemline — had Beth opted for a brief instead of shorts she would’ve been able to avoid this flashing moment.
