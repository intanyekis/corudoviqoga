Ezra Miller’s Many Controversies And Instances Of Erratic Behavior

Ezra Miller's reported inappropriate behavior is a constant source of criticism for him.

In 2022, the actor's arrests and criminal charges, which included disorderly behavior, harassment, and felony burglary, made news. There have also been rumors that the actor had an inappropriate relationship with minor activist Tokata Iron Eyes.

The North Dakota resident had a temporary order of protection issued against Miller by the Standing Rock Sioux tribal court. The youth leader claims in the protective order that the Flash actor "physically and emotionally harmed" her.

Several months before Iron Eyes' parents filed a lawsuit against Miller, the controversial actor was arrested in Hawaii in March 2022 and charged with disorderly conduct and harassment.

Based on a police report obtained by Page Six, the Fantastic Beasts actor was reportedly "yelling obscenities and became agitated when people began singing karaoke." After entering a plea of no contest, they were given a $500 fine.

Miller was arrested once more in Hawaii three weeks after the karaoke incident, this time on charges of throwing a chair at a woman's head. Yikes! The event occurred in a party at a private property in Pahoa on the Big Island of Hawaii, according to a press statement from the Hawaii Police Department.

“During the course of their investigation, police determined that the individual, later identified as Ezra Miller, became irate after being asked to leave and reportedly threw a chair, striking a 26-year-old female on the forehead, resulting in an approximate half-inch cut,” the release read.

What Happened Between Ezra Miller And Emma Watson?

In the film adaptation of The Perks of Being a Wallflower, Emma Watson, Logan Lerman, and Ezra Miller play three friends who, during the course of their friendship, face a variety of difficult challenges, such as grief, homosexuality, substance abuse, and mental health difficulties.

Miller told MTV that he loved his cast members so much that genuine friendships grew out of the chemistry each character had with the other.

“It is maybe the most miraculous and incredible thing that happened with the making of this movie is that we did no chemistry testing and somehow ended up all feeling like we had the relationships of our characters. It ended up in this situation of binding endless friendship that persists to this day. It’s a case of absolute serendipity and synchronicity. It just occurred that way. And that definitely was a lucky turn for us.”

In an old interview, they revealed that Watson was one of their "closest friends" and that they became fast friends while working together on the project. Miller claims that the two developed a close "brother and sister" relationship through sharing a hotel room next to each other.

This seems to be the case, since Miller claims that he immediately contacted his Perks of Being a Wallflower co-star Emma Watson after being cast in Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them. Beasts is a precursor to the Harry Potter film series, in which Watson has appeared since she was very young.

"Emma was the first person I called," Miller said with a chuckle during a New York press conference for the film.

"I think, usually, if you know someone who has worked with anyone who you are about to work with, it seems rational to check in and find out what you are plunging into. And, fortunately, when I called Emma, I was given good news. She told me what a magical experience it was for her.”

Despite their obvious closeness, it is unknown whether or not they still stay in touch or if Watson has begun to avoid Miller as a result of his negative publicity.
