The Best Moments From The Vogue Celebrity Beauty Secrets Series On YouTube

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Vogue has been a leading authority in fashion and beauty for decades, and their YouTube channel, Vogue Beauty Secrets, is no exception. This channel offers a behind-the-scenes look at the latest beauty trends, tips, and tricks from some of the biggest names in the industry. From makeup tutorials to skincare advice, there's something for everyone on this channel.

Related: The Best Celebrity Beauty Lines Of 2022We'll be highlighting some of the best moments from Vogue Beauty Secrets that you won't want to miss. From celebrity interviews to expert advice, these are the videos that have left a lasting impression on viewers. So sit back, relax, and get ready to take some notes as we take a look at the best moments from Vogue Beauty Secrets on YouTube.

10 Rihanna's Iconic 10-Minute Makeup Routine

Not only was Rihanna insanely fun and silly during the introduction of this video, but she was also so real when she talked about her weight gain and embracing the body she lives in. Her makeup came out beautifully and viewers got to see her beauty techniques from contouring to enhancing her eyes with complimenting eyeshadow tones.

9 Kendall Jenner's Tips And Tricks For Dealing With Acne-Prone Skin

Kendall Jenner's epic skincare routine gave everyone struggling with acne-prone skin hope. She started the video wearing a homemade avocado mask and explained the simple yet effective recipe. Not only was her skin glowing by the end of her skincare routine, but she also revealed her makeup routine that looked effortless yet stunning. Plus, the supermodel seemed super down to earth in her video, and she gave her sisters' brands so much support.

8 Brooke Shields On Anti-Aging Products

Brooke Shields was super informative during her Beauty Secrets video on Vogue's YouTube Channel. The 56-year-old supermodel dove into her favorite skincare lines that work great on mature skin and gave some tips on applying makeup to aging skin. It was also fascinating to hear her talk about the evolution of makeup from when she was an adolescent model to her career today. Even if you don't have mature skin, this one is definitely worth a watch!

7 Kate Moss And Her Guide To Cool Girl Makeup

It was very refreshing to hear Kate Moss Talk about her new, healthy lifestyle change at the beginning of the video which probably prompted millions of people to take better care of themselves. The model from the start grabbed the viewers' attention. Everything about Kate Moss' beauty routine is super simple yet so chic. She gifted us a sneak peek into her world of grunge makeup and her epic textured hair. And it's crazy to see how much Kate Moss hasn't really aged. It must be all the yoga and meditation!

6 Hailey Bieber Gave Insight On Her 'Glazed Donut' Skin

It's no secret that Hailey Bieber has one of the most beautiful complexions in the celebrity world. Her skin always looks dewy, glowy, and soft, so it's no wonder why her beauty secrets video has one of the higher view counts in relation to the other videos in the same playlist. Her skincare routine wasn't intimidating at all and her makeup steps were super easy to follow. Plus her personality shined through in the video as she discussed her jealousy of Justin Bieber's eyelashes and how her arms get tangled up during her beauty routine.

5 Selena Gomez And Her Go-To Evening Look

Selena Gomez discussed her Met Gala look and her hair transformations while revealing her favorite beauty products and regimens. We love that she threw in some essential aspects to getting ready that other celebrities didn't include in their videos, like lighting candles and playing your favorite tunes. Plus the actress showed us all why we should go out and buy every Rare Beauty product we can get our hands on because her makeup looked absolutely stunning.

4 Ellie Goldstein On Healthy Skin and Being Confident

Ellie Goldstein radiated with self-love and confidence in her Beauty Secrets with Vogue. Her message of staying true to yourself resonated with viewers and with those in the comment section. The model also kept her skincare routine quick and simple yet super informative. And let's not forget how important it is to hear perspectives from those with disabilities. Ellie Goldstein's Beauty Secrets segment is definitely one of the most impactful in the playlist.

3 Doja Cat's Iconic E-Girl Makeup Routine

Doja Cat broke the internet in December 2019 when her Beauty Secrets withVogue was released. It seemed like everyone on TikTok, YouTube, and other social media platforms wanted to try the singer's epic routine. Her video started so many different trends from applying blush nearly all over the face to using bronzer on the eyelids and directly over the cheeks. Her skincare and makeup hacks forced viewers to rethink the way they do their makeup and influenced them to break boundaries. Because let's face it, skincare and makeup is art and there's no "right way" to go about self-expression.

2 Grimes And Her Brutal Honesty And Crazy Cool Makeup Look

During Grimes' Beauty Secrets video, the singer was very open about pregnancy symptoms, how she sometimes makes herself look a tad bit too crazy with her stage makeup, and her experiences with her unibrow. Viewers could tell how artistic the singer is when she started doing her psychedelic makeup. Grimes was definitely extremely entertaining, and she taught us that you don't need to do your makeup like everyone else to feel confident.

1 Halsey Taught Vogue How To Achieve Her Album Cover Look

When Halsey released her album, Manic, many users across various social media platforms attempted to replicate the makeup look on the cover art. But when Halsey showed viewers which products she actually used to achieve the blue eye, people in the comments were thrilled. It was obvious how much Halsey loves beauty in the YouTube segment.She explained how artistic makeup is and how similar it is to painting on a canvas.It's always refreshing to be reminded that those in the beauty world are indeed artists.
