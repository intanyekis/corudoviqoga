Kelly Rowland's Son Titan Models in Outfits from Beyonc's Icy Park Collection See Photos

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Kelly Rowland's son Titan recently showed off his modeling skills in photos that featured him wearing outfits from Beyonce's Icy Park range.

Kelly Rowland's son Titan has all it takes to be a model. Recent photos of him are proof that he will excel in the modeling business should he venture into it.

The pictures, which were shared via his personal Instagram page, featured Titan posing up a storm while wearing pieces from Beyonce's Icy Park line.

Kelly Rowland at the Pizza Hut x Legion M Lounge on January 24, 2020 | Getty Images

Titan was dressed in the same outfit in two of the pictures— a grey hoodie with matching cargo pants complete with white sneakers. Both shots were taken outdoors, with the greenery contrasting with what the cute boy had on.

The last picture in the slideshow captured Titan wearing a more colorful piece from Queen Bee's fabulous collection as he lounged high up on a shelf. He captioned the post,

"I'm sooooo(sic) icy!!!!"

His fans clearly thought so too because there were only compliments for him in the comment section. Titan is one of the few children to have modeled Beyonce's limited-edition Icy Park line.

Prior to its launch in February, the line had already gone viral because top celebrities and their kids were modeling it. Beyonce's daughter Blue Ivy was one of those who featured in the clothing campaign.

The music icon was especially happy when Rowland announced her new child's birth.

Some might say that, like Titan, she is also a born model. Beyonce expressed her pride in Blue when she shared two photos from her shoot on her site.

Titan is Rowland's first child with her husband, Tim Weatherspoon. The two also share another son named Noah. They welcomed him earlier this year, and the proud mother made the announcement via social media.

She shared a photo of the baby and his older brother, who was looking down at him with a smile on his face. The same shot was also shared on Titan's Instagram page. The boy was happy to meet his new brother, but he wasn't the only one.
