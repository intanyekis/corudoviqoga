Who Is Zoe Giordano? How Old Is She?

Zoe Giordano Harrelson is a second daughter of the Hollywood Hunk, Woody. She is 25 years old as of 2021.

Zoe is two years younger than her elder sibling, Deni. Zoe was born in 1996. She celebrates her birthday every year on September 22.

She has already made her acting debut. She appeared with her dad in the 2015 short film U2: Song for Someone, as per The Huffington Post. In the same year, Zoe made a Ted Talk at her high school. She spoke about the importance of disconnecting from phones.

According to the YouTube video description, Zoe said that phones were removing us from reality and moments. Check out her video.

Know About Makani Ravello And Her Age

Makani Ravello Harrelson is 15 years old as of 2021. She was born in 2006 and celebrated her birthday every year on June 3. She is the youngest of the bunch.

After the birth of Makani, Woody and Laura said, “In this crazy patriarchal world we live in, we are doing our part to balance the energy. We are proud to announce the end of our goddess trilogy with the birth of our third daughter, Makani Ravello.”

Makani has hopes of maturing like a star like her father. She performed in her first movie in LBJ in 2016. One year later, she played a small role in Lost in London. She still has a long way to go.

Who Is Deni Montana Harrelson? Age Details Explored

Deni Montana Harrelson is the first child of Woody.

She was born on March 5, 1994. She is 27 years old. Some websites often claim that she arrived on February 28.

Woody has praised his eldest daughter for being strong and tough. She is a secluded person and stays away from the spotlight.

She has attended a few rare red carpet events with her famous father.

Woody and his wife homeschooled Deni during the early years of her life. But he regrets this as he used to be extremely busy with his Hollywood career. He added that he was not getting enough time with his child.
