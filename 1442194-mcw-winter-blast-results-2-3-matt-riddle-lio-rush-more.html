MCW Winter Blast Results (2/3): Matt Riddle, Lio Rush, More

Publish date: 2024-08-27

MCW Pro Wrestling kicked off their MCW Winter Blast tour from the Hollywood Volunteer Fire Department in Hollywood, Maryland, on February 3.

Matt Riddle, Lio Rush, and other stars competed at the show.

The results, courtesy of MCW Pro Wrestling, are as follows:

  • Ronnie & Ryan Zukko def. The All Naturals w/ Simone Valentina
  • “Miami” Mike Walker w/ Hellfire McQuire def. Alec Odin
  • The Hype def. Ykies & Chulo Montana, Diego Cruz & Lor Diaz & AK & Tony Macko
  • : Myles Hawkins (c) def.  Zakar Shaw, James Ellsworth, and Big Trouble Ben Bishop
  • Lio Rush def. “The Mecca” Brian Johnson
  • Bryan Idol & Natalia Markova w/ Cash Carter def. Jadis Quinn & Gia Scott
  • Ken Dixon Defeated Dick Dynamo
  • Simone Valentina & Lena Cross Defeated Ivy Malibu & Amara Voyd
  • MCW Heavyweight Champion Demarcus Kayne & Matt Riddle Defeated Spencer Slade & Big AC w/ Cash Carter
