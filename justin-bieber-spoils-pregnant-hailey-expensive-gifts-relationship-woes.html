Justin Bieber Spoils Pregnant Hailey With Expensive Gifts Amid Relationship Woes

Publish date: 2024-05-26

Highlights

  • Justin Bieber surprises Hailey with a $1.5 million engagement ring amid rumors, marking their vow renewal celebration.
  • Spectacular upgrade may also address Hailey's pregnancy-related finger swelling, leading to ring size adjustments.
  • Despite relationship speculation and mental health concerns, the couple remains committed, relying on faith and support.

Justin Bieber is apparently sparing no expense for his pregnant wife Hailey Bieber , opting to surprise her with a million-dollar ring despite persisting rumors of relationship problems between the couple.

Last month, the pop star and his model wife celebrated their baby-on-the-way by renewing their wedding vows in a picturesque ceremony in Hawaii. As part of the vow renewal festivities, Hailey apparently received a stunning new addition to her jewelry collection.

Justin Spent Millions On Hailey’s New Engagement Ring

Since the festivities, she’s been seen wearing a significantly larger diamond on her left hand since the celebration. This new piece is a major upgrade from Hailey's original engagement ring, an oval-shaped diamond designed by Solow & Co.

The new, impressive oval-shaped diamond ring was crafted by renowned celebrity jeweler Lorraine Schwartz. The diamond is set in a four-prong setting and paired with a yellow gold band, with the entire piece reportedly valued at $1.5 million.

This sparkling upgrade not only marks the couple's vow renewal but might also serve a practical purpose. Some women experience finger swelling during pregnancy, which could necessitate a different ring size.

Hailey's original engagement ring (estimated to be worth around $500,000) has been seen adorning her right pinky finger, suggesting another reason for the switch. Her initial engagement ring was also noteworthy for its single, large diamond. Though the exact size has never been officially confirmed, estimates suggest it ranges between six and ten carats.

Why Fans Are Worried For Justin’s Well-Being

While Justin and Hailey may have used the vow renewal as a way to public confirm their love, some fans remain worried about their marriage as well as Justin’s mental health.

"Justin has been facing some difficulties lately. He has been having a hard time and hasn't been feeling like his usual self," a source told Entertainment Tonight in late April.

They said Hailey has been doing everything in her power to be supportive, despite reports the couple are living separately while expecting their first baby. "Hailey has been doing her best to be there for Justin, but it is upsetting for her to see him struggle," the insider claimed. "They are doing their best to make things work and be supportive of each other. They are committed to doing whatever it takes to make things better."

Another report says the pop star has been relying on religion during the difficult time, saying a recent crying selfie Justin shared was the result of him feeling spiritually moved.

“Justin wears his emotions on his sleeve, and it is no secret that he is very religious. The pictures he put up of him crying may have prompted fans to question his well-being, and whether his marriage is on the rocks – but it was about his love of Jesus,” a source told Daily Mail.

For now, the Biebers haven’t commented on speculation on their marriage or Justin’s well-being. But Hailey is reportedly at least six months along.
