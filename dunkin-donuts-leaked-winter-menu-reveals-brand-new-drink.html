Dunkin Donuts Leaked Winter Menu Reveals Brand New Drink

Publish date: 2024-06-16

Dunkin’s winter menu has officially leaked, bringing a delicious harmony of beloved classics and tantalizing new additions. As reported by @Snackolator, a popular Instagram account dedicated to food news, this season will see the return of some delightful treats. Among them are the Pink Velvet Macchiato, Pancake Wake-Up Wraps, Sweet Black Pepper Bacon Sandwich, and Sweet Black Pepper Snacking Bacon.

Dunkin’ is now catering to the cravings of both hot and iced coffee lovers with the exciting addition of White Hazelnut Bark Coffee. Coffee fans can treat themselves to this highly anticipated beverage starting on December 27, the same day Dunkin’s new winter menu hits.

Dunkin’ is introducing the Frosted Red Velvet Donut, a specialty treat that features delicate cream cheese-flavored curls. Indulge in a bite of this delectable donut and relish its luscious, fluffy goodness, famously known as red velvet! Social media is abuzz with users eagerly anticipating Dunkin’s post-holiday special, brimming with joy and excitement.

Dunkin’ is also getting ready to launch its Valentine’s Day-themed treats, which will include the delectable Brownie Batter Donut and the delightful Cupid’s Choice. These mouthwatering menu items will be available starting from January 31st.

As people eagerly embrace the new offerings, there is an even greater excitement surrounding the return of their beloved Dunkin refreshers. Among these cherished favorites is the exquisite Pink Velvet Macchiato.

Meanwhile, during December, Starbucks is spreading joy to its customers by offering 50% off on any beverage every Thursday. The coffee chain recently announced this festive offer on Instagram. The cool deal is valid from noon to 6 p.m. local time. This promotion began on December 7th and will continue on the 21st, and 28th.

Starbucks just launched the Iced Gingerbread Oatmilk Chai, a festive drink that’s like a warm hug for your taste buds! This blend of gingerbread, chai spices, and creamy oat milk will have you sleighing through winter with a smile.

Starbucks’ much-awaited red cups made their grand entrance last month. However, this year, they’re sporting an unexpected twist: a vibrant magenta hue. Described by the chain as “mood-boosting,” these holiday cups embody the theme of “Share the Joy” with their modern and dynamic designs. Get ready to embrace the festive spirit as you sip your favorite Starbucks brew from these beautifully crafted cups.
