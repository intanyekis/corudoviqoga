Billie Eilish and Haley Kalil swap compliments at Golden Globes 2024

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Model Haley Kalil capped off her star-studded night at the 2024 Golden Globes with an epic Billie Eilish encounter.

While attending Sunday’s big show at The Beverly Hilton in Beverly Hills, the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit alumna — who split from former NFL lineman Matt Kalil in 2022 — crossed paths with Eilish, with the pair swapping compliments with one another.

“Billie, can I just say I am literally obsessed with you,” Kalil can be heard telling Eilish in a clip shared Monday on her Instagram page.

Eilish then replied, “Thanks, babe, you look hot as balls.”

“You look hot as balls,” responded Kalil, who wore a black gown with a plunging neckline. “I love her.”

Eilish became a two-time Golden Globe winner on Sunday after winning Best Original Song for “What Was I Made For?” from the Golden Globe-nominated film “Barbie.”

In addition to Eilish — who spoke candidly about her appreciation for women in a November interview with Variety — Kalil mingled with fellow Golden Globe nominee Elizabeth Olsen and also caught Taylor Swift making her way down the red carpet.

Kalil, an influencer boasting 3.7 million Instagram followers, learned just days ago that she was Golden Globes-bound.

“Never in a million years did I ever think I would be walking the Golden Globes red carpet next to people like Taylor Swift and Selena Gomez, but here we are,” she said in a “get ready with me” video on social media.

“This all started like four days ago when I get a call from somebody at Dick Clark Productions, which is the production company that puts on the Globes. He says, ‘How do you feel about going to the Golden Globes?’ I about peed myself.”

Kalil burst onto the scene after being named a co-winner of Sports Illustrated Swimsuit’s first-ever SwimSearch with Camille Kostek in 2018.

She filed for divorce from husband Matt in May 2022. They wed in 2015.
